Ouru/Oul A tall young man who is one of the main characters of the story. He was originally an angel with a serious heart & compassion for others, but in the past he could no longer endure the tyranny of Jupiter in Heaven and turned away and fell to Earth. Although he felt guilty about this, he made ends meet by working as a musician, which is unpopular in this world. After meeting Yuli, who was able to understand and show each other's weaknesses, they lived a happy life despite being poor, but his story progressed when he disappeared from this world due to Pluto's plot.
Yuli/Yuri/Yuuri An odd-eyed girl who is one of the main characters of the story. She has a quiet and kind personality, but has low self-esteem and is prone to anxiety. She was born as a half-human demon from hell, but currently neither of her parents are with her. Ever since she was young, when she gets very emotional, her demon characteristics in her body, which caused her to lots of sadness, and when she was feeling lonely and despairing about life due to that experience and complex, she met Oul and gradually began to learn how to live.
Pluto He's of the beings that governs Hell, and although he looks like a child, he gives off a sense of bottomless dignity and power. He has a free-spirited personality, and handles large festivals & court proceedings to a moderate degree & basically leaves miscellaneous tasks and personal matters to his subordinates & seeks the pleasure of satisfying his solitude and thirst every day within his mansion. Periodically, he kidnapped the residents of Hell and made them live in his mansion & had them as playmates without any problems.
Jupiter She's one of the beings that governs Heaven & has an elegant yet intimidating appearance. Although he became a ruler only recently, she has completely changed the security and appearance of Heaven with his bottomless malice & overwhelming power. She takes pleasure in implementing ways to torment beings other than himself who have wills and emotions, especially sinners who have no consciousness of guilt. She became interested in Oul, who betrayed him & fell into Heaven & chatted with her bestie, Pluto. He invites Oul, who wants to get Yuli back but has no way to do it, to his home, Heaven, and welcomes him.
